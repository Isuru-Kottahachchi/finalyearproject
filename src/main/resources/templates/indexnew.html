About

SMART EduLanka is a great platform for students who are struggling to self-study and improve their knowledge..

2022 BEST Online platform to learn and evaluate

Welcome to SmartEDU Lanka website

The ultimate goal of SMART EduLanaka is to help out students who are seeking more knowledge and trying to improve themselves.

This web-based system is developed and presented by the University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka. By using this website, students can ask their questions and get accurate answers. There are plenty of learning materials available here for self-study.

All contents are easy to understand and easy to read

We are always considering the quality of the content, and we always expect your opinions and suggestions. This system allows you to upload new valuable educational content. Our qualified and friendly admins will review and add your content to the system and students can save their valuable time using this system.

Students can save thier valubale time using this system.
